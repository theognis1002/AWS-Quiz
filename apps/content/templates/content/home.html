Introduction

IT certifications are highly sought after these days with virtually every company relying on the cloud. With AWS being the clear market leader in the cloud, it is a given for any person that wants to take the next step in their career to go for an AWS certification.

There are many study guides and practice exams out there but most are all throttled with ads, captchas, and paywalls. Due to frustration while studying for my Cloud Practitioner exam, I decided to create my own study guide to solve this issue. I am now making it available for others to take advantage of now!
